Download the 10-week program outlining key learning activities for the Bedside Table project.

Weekly Outline

  1. Week 1: Safety induction and design brief
  2. Week 2: Research and concept sketches
  3. Week 3: Working drawings and cutting list
  4. Week 4: Prepare timber and mark out components
  5. Week 5: Construct table frame
  6. Week 6: Fit top and drawer or shelf supports
  7. Week 7: Build drawer or shelf
  8. Week 8: Final assembly and hardware
  9. Week 9: Surface preparation and finishing
  10. Week 10: Evaluation and submission
