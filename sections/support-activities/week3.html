Week 5: Bandsaw Safety and Mortise and Tenon Accuracy

Mastering the bandsaw and refining mortise and tenon joints are essential skills. Ensure:

  • Bandsaw Safety: Keep blade guides just above the timber, feed timber steadily, and always wear PPE.
  • Joinery Accuracy: Accurate marking and careful cutting ensure tight, durable joints.

Practical Activity

  • Safely practise cutting tenons with a bandsaw.
  • Refine mortise and tenon joints for optimal fit.

Week 6: Bandsaw SOP and Mortise and Tenon Adjustments

Adhering to standard operating procedures (SOPs) ensures safety and consistency:

  • Bandsaw SOP: Regular blade checks, accurate blade guide positioning, steady feed rates.
  • Joint Adjustments: Accurate adjustments to ensure precise mortise and tenon fit.

Practical Activity

  • Follow SOPs closely for bandsaw operation.
  • Carefully adjust and perfect your mortise and tenon joints.
