Week 7: Thicknesser SOP and Orthogonal Drawings

Efficient timber preparation and clear drawings are critical for precise construction:

  • Thicknesser SOP: Remove material gradually, feed timber with the grain, and ensure blades are sharp.
  • Orthogonal Drawings: Provide accurate front, top, and side views with clear annotations.

Practical Activity

  • Safely operate the thicknesser to achieve correct timber dimensions.
  • Create detailed orthogonal drawings for your bedside table.

Week 8: Dry Clamping, Work Method Statements (WMS), and SOP Review

Dry clamping and clear method statements ensure smooth assembly and safe practice:

  • Dry Clamping: Test the assembly without glue to identify issues.
  • Work Method Statements: Outline task sequences, safety measures, and required PPE.
  • Review SOPs: Reinforce safe and effective operating procedures.

Practical Activity

  • Conduct a dry clamp of your bedside table.
  • Develop detailed Work Method Statements for project tasks.
