Week 20: Final Submission and Workshop Reflection

Theory Content

In Week 20, you will conclude your bedside table project by submitting your final product and comprehensive portfolio for assessment. Additionally, you'll reflect on your learning experiences and contributions to maintaining a safe and productive workshop environment.

Final Submission

Ensure your submission meets all project criteria:

  • Product Quality: Inspect your bedside table for structural integrity, finish quality, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.
  • Comprehensive Portfolio: Include all design sketches, CAD drawings, photographs, costings, evaluations, and reflective statements.
  • Timeliness: Submit your project and documentation within the designated deadline.

Workshop Reflection

Reflecting on your experiences fosters continuous improvement:

  • Identify skills developed, areas of significant improvement, and aspects you found challenging.
  • Evaluate how effectively you managed your time and resources throughout the project.
  • Reflect on your adherence to safety and sustainable practices.

Workshop Maintenance Review

Revisit your responsibility towards the workshop:

  • Verify the cleanliness, organisation, and readiness of tools and machinery for future use.
  • Confirm all materials and chemicals are properly stored and labelled.

Practical Activity

  • Complete a detailed reflection on your woodworking journey, highlighting personal growth and achievements.
  • Submit your finished bedside table and fully completed portfolio.
  • Conduct a final check of the workshop, ensuring it is clean, safe, and well-organised.
