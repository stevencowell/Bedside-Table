Week 11: Assembly, Gluing Techniques, and Costing

Theory Content

Week 11 marks the critical assembly phase of your bedside table project. Accurate assembly and proper gluing techniques ensure the structural integrity and durability of your furniture. Additionally, you will explore effective methods for calculating material costs, crucial for efficient project management.

Assembly and Gluing Techniques

Proper assembly and gluing involve:

  • Ensuring accurate alignment of all joints and components.
  • Applying glue evenly and sparingly to avoid squeeze-out.
  • Utilising appropriate clamping techniques to ensure even pressure.
  • Allowing sufficient drying time before moving or further processing.

Costing

Efficient cost management is key to successful woodworking projects. Consider:

  • Calculating the quantity of timber and materials accurately.
  • Accounting for all hardware, consumables, and finish materials.
  • Using spreadsheets or digital tools for precise costing calculations.

Practical Activity

  • Complete the assembly and gluing of your bedside table, carefully checking alignment.
  • Develop a detailed costing spreadsheet for your project.
