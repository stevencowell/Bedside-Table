Week 18: Class Test and Review of Key Concepts

Theory Content

In Week 18, you will undertake a comprehensive class test to assess your understanding and application of the knowledge and skills developed throughout the bedside table project. This test covers all key concepts, including safety, joinery, finishing techniques, sustainability, and project evaluation.

Revision Focus Areas

  • Workplace Health and Safety (WHS): Review PPE, safe machine operation, and emergency procedures.
  • Joinery Techniques: Understand and recall specifics of mortise and tenon, dovetails, and other joinery methods.
  • Material Preparation: Recall the FEWTEL method, timber dressing, and accurate measurement practices.
  • Finishing Techniques: Review surface preparation, various finishing methods, and safe chemical handling.
  • Sustainability Practices: Revise sustainable timber sourcing and waste reduction strategies.
  • Project Management: Recall sequencing, time planning, costing methods, and effective use of project management tools such as Gantt charts.

Practical Activity

  • Complete revision tasks focused on each key concept area to reinforce understanding.
  • Participate actively in revision discussions to clarify concepts and techniques.

Class Test Preparation

Review your notes, practical experiences, and previous quiz questions. Use provided resources and recommended external materials to consolidate your learning.
