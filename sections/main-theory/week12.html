Week 12: Assembly, Gluing, Sequencing, and Time Planning

Theory Content

In Week 12, you will finalise your assembly and gluing processes while learning effective sequencing and time planning strategies. These techniques are critical for efficient workflow management, ensuring timely and organised project completion.

Advanced Assembly and Gluing

  • Ensure all components are clean, correctly marked, and ready for assembly.
  • Use appropriate clamping pressure and allow sufficient curing time to achieve optimal joint strength.
  • Regularly check alignment and squareness during the gluing process to maintain structural integrity.

Sequencing and Time Planning

Effective sequencing involves organising project steps logically to improve workflow:

  • Identify all project tasks clearly.
  • Arrange tasks in logical order, considering dependencies and curing times.

Time planning involves accurately estimating the duration required for each task:

  • Use Gantt charts or similar project management tools to visualise your project timeline.
  • Allocate adequate time buffers for unexpected delays or adjustments.

Practical Activity

  • Complete final assembly, gluing, and clamping procedures.
  • Develop a detailed Gantt chart to manage your remaining project tasks effectively.
