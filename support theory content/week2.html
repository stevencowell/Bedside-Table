Weeks 3 & 4: Timber Preparation, Mortising, and Joinery

Week 3: Dressing Timber (DAR and FEWTEL)

Accurate timber preparation is essential for precise joinery and effective assembly. Understand these methods:

  • DAR (Dressed All Round): Ensuring all timber sides are smooth and square.
  • FEWTEL Method: Systematic timber preparation—Face, Edge, Width, Thickness, End, and Length.

Practical Activity

  • Practise dressing timber using hand planes and thicknessers.
  • Apply FEWTEL method to accurately prepare your timber.

Week 4: Mortiser Operation and Mortise and Tenon Joinery

Understanding mortise and tenon joinery ensures your project's structural integrity:

  • Mortiser Usage: Correctly operating machinery to create precise mortises.
  • Mortise and Tenon Joints: A strong, traditional woodworking joint essential for frame construction.

Practical Activity

  • Practise using a mortiser safely and accurately.
  • Create precise mortise and tenon joints and test their fit.
