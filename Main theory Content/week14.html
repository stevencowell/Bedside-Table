Week 14: Advanced Finishing Techniques and Industry Visit Preparation

Theory Content

Week 14 covers advanced finishing techniques that further enhance the quality and appeal of your bedside table. Additionally, you'll prepare for an industry visit to gain real-world insights into professional woodworking practices.

Advanced Finishing Techniques

Advanced finishes provide superior protection and aesthetic refinement:

  • French Polishing: Achieves high-gloss, elegant finishes suitable for quality furniture.
  • Spray Finishing: Provides even coverage and efficient application for professional-grade results.
  • Buffing and Waxing: Adds final polish, enhancing tactile and visual qualities.

Always apply advanced finishes with meticulous surface preparation and safety measures.

Industry Visit Preparation

  • Research the business you will visit.
  • Prepare relevant questions about professional woodworking practices, equipment, sustainability measures, and finishing techniques.
  • Observe workflow, machinery usage, and workplace safety practices during the visit.

Practical Activity

  • Experiment with an advanced finishing technique such as French polishing or spray finishing.
  • Develop a list of detailed questions for your industry visit to maximise learning.
